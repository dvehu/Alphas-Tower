About Us

Since 2018, the passion to advocate for a safe environment has been burning like wildfire. This gave birth to Alpha's Tower Environmental Concept Ltd in 2021. We accepted the mandate to urgently support the mission of protecting our environment.

Having recognized among others the emergence of environmental pollutants, increasing outbreak of disease as a result of poorly managed environment and low level of awareness on public environmental health practices, we are sold out to standing in-the gap.

We are a fast growing organization who strive for excellence and have reached out to a hundred plus satisfied client with our programs, products and services within 12 months. Our client base includes but not limited to food companies, government facilities, private organization, schools, hotels, recreational centres, hospitals, manufacturing industries, recycling industries, farms, residence apartments etc.

Mission Statement

"Consistently providing environmental best practices in programs, products and services to attain a safe environment for all."
